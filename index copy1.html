  • 1 - WHO WE ARE

    Born in Mumbai's golden glow, our story began with a single vertical blind—a humble stroke in a canvas of light. In the quiet of the 1990s, we worked with only a handful of shades, our hands learning the language of fabric and sun. Back then, every texture was imported, every design borrowed. But we saw what others didn’t, the potential to redefine the very fabric of window coverings.
